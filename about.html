About Us

Lebanese Food Emporium loves Lebanese food… We all love Lebanese food. Lebanese Food, brings our families together!

We are a Newcastle, NSW family-owned small business, dedicated to sourcing, manufacturing and supplying the highest quality Lebanese fresh roasted nuts, Lebanese dips and cheeses, fresh meats and chicken, Legumes and Grains, Health Foods, Confectionery, Snack Foods and herbs and Spices and much more. We deliver direct to your door, fresh kafta mince, kebab skewers, diced lamb and chicken breast, just to name a few.

We are proud to share our family business with you.

Sarah Haddad is the passionate creator of ‘Lebanese Food Emporium’, primarily an online Lebanese grocery store that delivers to your door.

Upon moving to Newcastle from Sydney, 4 years ago Sarah and her family quickly learnt that there was not much availability of Middle Eastern cuisine in the way of restaurants, take-aways, groceries and products. More importantly there where literally no Middle Eastern grocery store or bakery servicing the entire Central Coast, Lake Macquarie, Newcastle, Port Stephens and further to the Northern NSW.

Sarah, being an active mum in the community, soon had her realisations confirmed through certain online mothers’ groups and continual friendly locals stating the same facts.

Whenever Sarah’s family would visit from Sydney to Newcastle, Sarah kindly had to ask them to cart along a tone of Lebanese groceries and fresh Lebanese bread for her family. Sarah’s investigations indicated that there were many families living along the Central to North Coast getting by in the same way she was. Not only that, but many people also simply love Middle Eastern foods and grocery and were patiently waiting for someone to open up shop near them.

Our fascination for delicious Lebanese flavours, distinctive aromas, exotic tastes and unique textures are what drive us, along with our commitment to outstanding quality and service.

Our team has been bringing you our extensive array of Lebanese grocery and Lebanese products since 2020. With over 18 years’ combined experience in the industry, we are ready and willing to share our knowledge and passion for food, and all things Lebanese with you!

Our family is tirelessly involved in every aspect of our daily operations from ordering to delivery, ensuring a hands-on and personalized touch for an exemplary level of service to you, our valued customer.

Our aspiration is to be Australia’s largest stockist and most iconic, wholesaler and retailer of the finest quality Lebanese groceries and products, delivering health and happiness to you and your family!

We at the Lebanese Food Emporium, pride ourselves on prioritising Australian made fresh products used in manufacturing Lebanese cuisine and supporting local Australian farmers. We also have daily concern with the ongoing current situation in Lebanon and the Middle East, therefore we are trying to expand our product range to incorporate as many imported dry goods and products to assist on ground level. We also carefully select the finest ingredients from all over the world. Our product lines are vast so if there are products you cannot see on our site then just ask!

Through ‘Lebanese Food Emporium’ Sarah also provides a health food, Lebanese lifestyle blog. Sarah also shares delicious Lebanese recipes, jokes, music and even interesting facts about Lebanon. Also available on ‘The Lebanese Food Emporium’ site is an array of Lebanese DVD’S, music, religious items, homewares, Tumbaki drums, traditional folk games, merchandise and apparel and let’s not forget the Argilli.

By emphasis all things Lebanese and by specialising in all Lebanese grocery types, Sarah has built a growing audience of loyal door to door customers who crave authentic Lebanese food and products for their families along with friendly service as customers turn into friends.

Sarah has been hands on in working feverishly on her website… “I had many inspiring comments sent in to me, when the business was 1st established and ordering was via a simple spread sheet on Facebook. One popular comment I received time and time again was from non-Arabic customers (believe it or not), stating “if only you had a description and photos of products and groceries I’d buy more!” So, I have built a website that’s easy to navigate, full of in-depth product descriptions, loads of photos and even a ‘What is this product?’ clickable description.”

Sarah is a personal trainer and model who also once walked the limelight at the Miss Lebanon Australia and Lebanon beauty pageants as runner up. Sarah has a long history of creating successful start-up companies and business under her portfolio. Sarah was director of Everpure Health for over 11 years, one of Australia’s innovative and most respected natural healing products distributer nationally and internationally.

Sarah has appeared on TV and been featured in various publications as well as writing her own blogs content which appear on her sites.

Sarah has a passion for helping others reach similar goals in their lives and has mentored many successful entrepreneurs.

Connect with Sarah directly through The Lebanese Food Emporium on Instagram, Facebook, linked in and Twitter or stay on top of her latest posts by subscribing to her free newsletter. You can also email her at: lebanesegroceries2u@gmail.com
