Shipping & Delivery

You acknowledge that the Purchase Services offered by LEBANESE FOOD EMPORIUM the ‘Delivery Services’ through the use of their own delivery services (the ‘Delivery Service Providers’). In the event that an item is lost or damaged in the course of the Delivery Services, LEBANESE FOOD EMPORIUM asks that you are to inspect the items upon delivery and let the delivery person know right away.

If we expect a delay in your order delivery, we will notify you (email and/or Phone) about your order status and expected delivery time, as soon as we can. All deliveries are made to the address as given by you, and you will be available in person to receive the delivery.

If you are not available during delivery, we can leave the order at your door if we have your written consent to do so prior to delivery. We will not be responsible if the parcel is reported as missing after delivering the order at your door with your written consent. If you are not available to receive the delivery and we have not received your written consent to leave the delivery at your door, then we will have to re-schedule the delivery. You will be paying for the delivery charges. Delivery charges are based on your delivery suburb. ($15 or more).

If your delivery is to be left where you have given consent, delivery driver will take a photo of where your order was left as proof of delivery. This will be sent to the customer and kept on file by LEBANESE FOOD EMPORIUM.
